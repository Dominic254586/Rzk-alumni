About Us

The Riziki Alumni Network is a place for former residents, students, and staff of Riziki Children’s Home to connect, grow, and give back. Whether you were here as a child or have supported the journey of others — you are family.

Our platform fosters unity, mentorship, storytelling, and impact. Here, the past meets the future in a beautiful community built on love, sacrifice, and resilience.

Our Core Values

  • Love: We are bonded by care and compassion.
  • Service: Every alumni contributes in their own way.
  • Gratitude: We never forget where we came from.
  • Hope: We light the way for the next generation.

Be Part of the Family

Join us as we continue the legacy of Riziki. Whether you're here to reconnect, mentor, support, or be supported — welcome home.
