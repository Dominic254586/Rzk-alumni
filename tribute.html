Mentors Who Shaped Us

Pa Julius Kivindyo

More than a director — a father figure, teacher, and disciplinarian whose wisdom echoed through every corridor. “Heri nibaki na mtu mmoja ambaye ako tayari kutii...” — your voice still guides us.

Aunty Hellen

Her hugs could heal. Her words could lift. Aunty Hellen’s unwavering love and support kept many of us going even when we had nothing left. You are forever family.

Teacher Shonnie

A true gem, not just within Riziki but beyond. She walked with us even after school hours and after Riziki days were done. Your heart is felt in every success we have.

Alumni We Celebrate

Dennis Kibet

A basketball maestro whose passion lit up the court and inspired others to rise. You played with purpose, pride, and humility. We salute your skill and spirit.
